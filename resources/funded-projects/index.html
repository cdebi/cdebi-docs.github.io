Funded Projects¶

Research Grants¶

The 38 research grants funded as of May 2014 addressed all four Phase 1 C-DEBI research themes (many address multiple themes). Almost half are associated with a C-DEBI Major Program at the Juan de Fuca Ridge Flank, South Pacific Gyre, North Pond, and Dorado, and many projects have been associated with other drilling expeditions or not at all.
